# User Guide

This guide will help you get started using Valero as a content creator.

## üöÄ Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. Visit the registration page
2. Fill in your details:
   - Name
   - Email address
   - Password
3. Verify your email address
4. Log in to your account

### First Login

After logging in, you'll see:
- **Dashboard** - Overview of your content
- **Articles** - Your articles list
- **Profile** - Your account settings

## üìù Creating Articles

### Basic Article Creation

1. Navigate to **Articles ‚Üí Create New**
2. Fill in the basic information:
   - **Title** - Your article title
   - **Content** - Use the rich text editor
   - **Category** - Select or create a category
   - **Publish Date** - Choose when to publish

### Using the Rich Text Editor

The editor supports:
- **Bold**, *Italic*, and other formatting
- Headings (H1-H6)
- Lists (ordered and unordered)
- Links
- Images
- Tables
- Code blocks

**Smart Paste**: When pasting content, the editor preserves:
- Lists and formatting
- Indentation
- Basic styling

### Adding Images

#### Upload New Images

1. Go to the **Gallery** tab
2. Click **Upload Images** or drag and drop
3. Select up to 30 images
4. Images appear with previews
5. Set a cover image by clicking the star icon

#### Use Existing Images

1. Click **Select from Media Library**
2. Browse your existing images
3. Search or filter if needed
4. Select images to reuse
5. Click **Attach Selected**

### Setting Cover Image

1. Upload or select an image
2. Click the **star icon** on the image
3. The image becomes the cover
4. Only one cover image per article

### Article Options

In the **Options** tab, you can:

#### External Links
- **YouTube Link** - Add YouTube video URL
- **Instagram Link** - Add Instagram post URL
- **Local Store Link** - Add store/product URL

#### Bookmarks Library
- Search your saved bookmarks
- Filter by category
- Copy links with one click
- View notes for each bookmark

### SEO Settings

In the **SEO** tab:
- **Meta Title** - SEO title (recommended: 50-60 characters)
- **Meta Description** - SEO description (recommended: 150-160 characters)
- **Keywords** - Comma-separated keywords
- **OG Image** - Social media preview image

The SEO tab shows:
- Character counts
- Validation warnings
- SEO score

### FAQ Automatic Schema
 
To automatically generate a specific FAQ schema for Google:
- Write your content in the formatted: `Q: Your Question? A: Your Answer.`
- Elements grouped in headings or bold text are automatically detected.
- Example: `<h3>Q: What is this?</h3> <p>This is Valero.</p>` or `<strong>Q: Is it free?</strong> <br> A: Yes!`

### Scheduling Articles

1. Set a **Publish Date** in the future
2. The article becomes scheduled
3. View scheduled articles in **Articles ‚Üí Scheduled**
4. Preview scheduled articles before publishing
5. Edit or delete scheduled articles anytime

## üìö Managing Articles

### Article List

View all your articles:
- **Published** - Live articles
- **Drafts** - Unpublished articles
- **Scheduled** - Future articles

### Editing Articles

1. Click **Edit** on any article
2. Make your changes
3. Click **Update Article**
4. Changes are saved immediately

### Deleting Articles

1. Click **Delete** on an article
2. Confirm deletion
3. **Note**: Shared images are preserved for other articles

## üîñ Using Bookmarks

### Creating Bookmarks

1. Go to **Articles Menu ‚Üí Bookmarks**
2. Click **New Bookmark**
3. Fill in:
   - **Title** - Bookmark name
   - **Link** - URL (optional)
   - **Notes** - Additional information
   - **Category** - Organize by category
4. Click **Save Bookmark**

### Using Bookmarks in Articles

1. While creating/editing an article
2. Go to **Options** tab
3. Scroll to **Bookmarks Library**
4. Search or filter bookmarks
5. Click **Copy Link** to copy the URL
6. Paste into your article content

### Managing Bookmarks

- **Edit** - Update bookmark details
- **Delete** - Remove bookmarks
- **Filter** - Filter by category
- **Search** - Search across all fields
